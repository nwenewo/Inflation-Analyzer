# 🇳🇬 Inflation Impact Analyzer — AI-Powered Cost of Living Tool

## 💡 Overview

**Inflation is silently eating into Nigerians' wallets.**  
This AI-powered Streamlit app helps users **understand the real impact of inflation on their monthly expenses** and offers **smart, culturally relevant saving tips** — tailored to their income, city, and lifestyle.

> Built as part of the **#3MTT AI-Powered Solutions Showcase (July 2025)**.


## 🚀 Features

✅ Input your:
- Monthly income  
- City or state in Nigeria  
- Lifestyle level (Basic, Moderate, Comfortable)  
- Inflation scenario (Low, Medium, High)

✅ Instantly see:
- Adjusted monthly expenses under inflation
- Breakdown by food, transport, housing, utilities, and misc.
- Whether you're running a **surplus or deficit**

✅ 💡 Get AI-Powered Suggestions:
- Personalized, local, practical cost-saving advice
- Powered by **OpenAI's GPT-4o** for Nigerian users
